What do we do

Tenantverify.in aims to simplify background checks and due diligence of Tenants, Paying Guest, Domestic Help, Housekeeping, Gardeners, Security Guards, Cooks, Drivers and Employees of all IT and Non IT sector.
  • India is a country with 1.38 billion people.
  • India is now a major IT power too
However, when it comes to the area of background checks and due diligence, there is a lot of work to be done. Tenantverify.in, is dedicated to bridging this gap.
  • Democratize the background verification process and bring it within easy reach of every individual.
    • To achieve these, Tenantverify.in has brought together a great team covering the diverse areas of Data Engineering, Online Business, Real Estate and Background Checks. This team is passionate about applying the technology to solve the complex problems in background checks that are unique to Indian scenario.
    Our Strategic Advisory Board includes distinguished personalities from academia and research, defence and investigative areas, web and mobile businesses. The Strategic Advisory Board guides our board with right direction and roadmap.
    Our name ‘Tenantverify.in’ was coined by Mr. Anuj Jain, CEO, of the company. Our tagline ‘ Verify today Secure tomorrow’ captures the essence of what we offer – providing background verification for Tenants, Paying Guest, Domestic Help, Housekeeping, Gardeners, Security Guards, Cooks, Drivers and Employees of all IT and Non IT sector.
