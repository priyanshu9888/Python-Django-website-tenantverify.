What is Tenantverify.in ?

We help Societies, Landlords, Property Managers and Employers to verify the background of their Tenants, PGs, Domestic helps, Housekeeping, Gardeners, Security Guards, Cooks, Drivers and Employees of all other sector to achieve a greater sense of confidence when allowing them to rent their place or employ them. . .

Allowing a new resident or employee into your property by having factual information at hand about the individual can help owners or employers make a better, more educated and rational decision.
