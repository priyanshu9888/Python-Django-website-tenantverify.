How does Tenantverify.in work?

Documents Collection

We collect documents from your doorsteps and verify them before submitting to the Police Department.

Acknowledgement Receipt

We deliver the Police Verification acknowledgement receipt post submission of documents at your doorsteps.

Appointment

We guide the verifier for physical appearance in the local police station as per accurate process

Report Delivery

Finally, we deliver the Police Verification Certificate issued by Police Department at your doorsteps.
